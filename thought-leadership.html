FAQ

Questions, answered.

How is this different from other content offerings?+

Most services generate content from nothing, which is why it reads as generic. We do the opposite:

  • → A brand brain built on your real interviews, sales-call language and positioning first
  • → Every output governed by your foundational documents and a human review pass
  • → A source of truth that knows your brand, not posts you buy off a template
What exactly is a "brand brain"?+

A governed knowledge base loaded with your voice, positioning, proof, frameworks and ICP, run by five foundational documents. It knows your brand better than anyone and produces sharp, on-brand assets across every channel, organic and paid.

How long until it's live?+

Typically 4-6 weeks across three phases: voice calibration, first batch, approve & launch. The timeline depends on your turnaround on the intake form, calibration feedback and Month 1 inputs.

Can I post on a Personal Page instead of a Company Page?+

Yes. Founder and expert profiles are usually the strongest trust signal, which is why the brand brain is built around individual founder and expert voices. We support company-page publishing too.

How does this connect to our paid ads?+

Directly. We test messages organically, amplify the winners as thought-leader ads across LinkedIn, YouTube and Facebook, then harvest the signals: organic engagement, named website visitors and ad engagement. Those flow into email and LinkedIn connect and message plays that feed the whole funnel.
