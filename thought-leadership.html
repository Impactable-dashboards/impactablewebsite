FAQ

Questions, answered.

How is this different from other content offerings?+

Most services generate from nothing, which is why it reads as generic AI. We do the opposite:

  • → A context environment fed by your real interviews, sales language and positioning first
  • → Every output guarded with human review
  • → A source of truth your team can interact with, not posts you buy
What exactly is a "Brand Engine"?+

A living AI context environment loaded with your voice, positioning, proof, frameworks and ICP. It's the single source of truth for everything you publish, stays current, and can be queried by your team on demand.

How long until it's live?+

Typically 4-6 weeks across three phases: voice calibration, first batch, approve & launch. The timeline depends on your turnaround on the intake form, calibration feedback and Month 1 inputs.

Can I post on a Personal Page instead of a Company Page?+

Yes. Founder and expert profiles are usually the strongest trust signal, which is why the engine is built around individual voices. We support company-page publishing too.

How does this connect to our paid ads?+

Directly. We test messages organically, amplify winners as thought-leader ads, then qualify engagement on LinkedIn, name accounts, and route them into the warm pool that feeds search, email and the swarm.
