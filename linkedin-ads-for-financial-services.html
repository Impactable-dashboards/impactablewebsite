The financial-services buyer and white space

In financial services, trust is the whole sale, and everyone claims it.

01

The saturated lane

"Trusted," "secure," "compliant," "enterprise-grade." Table stakes, not differentiation. The open lane is specific, provable outcomes for a named buyer.

02

The real committee

The VP or director who owns the initiative, risk and compliance who can stop it, and the economic buyer who funds it. Nothing moves without compliance comfortable.

03

The motion

Cautious, long, and reference-driven. Credibility and proof content carry more weight than volume.
