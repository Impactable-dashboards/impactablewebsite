Why signal beats spray

Cold outreach is a numbers game. Activation is a signal game.

The difference isn't effort, it's timing. Reach an account the week it visited your pricing page or engaged your founder's post, and the same message lands completely differently. We let the signals decide who to reach and when, so the sequence works with intent instead of against it. Same team, same tools, far better odds.
