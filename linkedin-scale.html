Why running accounts stall

Six things quietly cap the account.

At six-figure budgets, each one is expensive. The Impact Report finds all six.

✕

Stuck on the lead-gen treadmill

Bottom-funnel content downloads that produce volume but stall before sales. The fix that drove Lacework's 6X was full-funnel, not more gated PDFs.

✕

Budget leaking to the wrong segments

At $100k a month, 20% waste is $20k a month. Most accounts can't see which industries, sizes, and seniorities are absorbing spend with zero return.

✕

Flying blind on account signals

The companies clicking three, five, ten times never reach sales while intent is hot. No signal layer, no account intelligence, no plays.

✕

Reporting clicks, not revenue

Dashboards full of CTR and CPM that can't answer the board's question: what pipeline and revenue did this spend actually influence?

✕

A generalist agency on a specialist channel

LinkedIn treated as one more line item by a junior team, with no proprietary tooling for day-parting, suppression, or frequency control.

✕

Plateaued, with no path to the next stage

Performance flattened and nobody can say why, or what actually gets them to the next level of scale.
