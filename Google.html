The problem with paid search in isolation

Google alone is a leaky bucket.

Search captures intent, and that intent is valuable. But run in a silo, the click is expensive and competitive, your prospect is comparing three or four competitors in the same session, and everything rides on a single landing page visit. Most never come back. You end up paying premium prices for traffic that bounces, and standalone ROI gets hard to prove. The fix is not more budget. It is a system that catches what search brings in.

Why Google + LinkedIn

Each channel's weakness is the other's strength.

Neither channel proves ROI alone. Together, the attribution loop closes. This is the core of how we run paid search: not as a standalone line item, but as the capture layer of a system LinkedIn qualifies and nurtures.

The mechanism

Capture, qualify, compound.

Here is how it works in practice. Imagine a month of Google Ads driving in-market traffic to your site. On its own, most of it bounces. Inside the system, it becomes a qualified, retargetable, compounding asset.

1

Capture the intent

Google brings the buyers actively searching: your branded terms, competitor terms, and bottom-of-funnel solution phrases. The warmest traffic you can buy.

2

Qualify the click

The LinkedIn Insight Tag sees all of that traffic, then filters it by company size, industry, title, seniority, and ABM lists, so spend focuses only on good-fit accounts instead of every random visitor.

3

Compound the demand

Qualified visitors feed retargeting pools, Meta and programmatic reinforce the message, and accounts exposed on both Google and LinkedIn accelerate through the pipeline.

How we run it

Paid search, run like it matters.

Anyone can do quick keyword research and hand you a campaign. Mature management is about what happens every week after launch, and how search connects to everything else you run.

Intent first

Start at the bottom

We begin with high-converting, bottom-of-funnel core phrases, not a wide net for search volume. We aim for the searches ready to convert, then expand from proof.

Defense

Own your name and theirs

Protect your branded terms so competitors cannot rank ahead of you, and bid on competitor terms to win prospects in the awareness and consideration stages.

Fuel

Build pools, not just clicks

Every qualified visit becomes retargeting fuel for LinkedIn, Meta, and programmatic. Search is the top of the conversion path, not the end of it.

Conversion

Pages and Quality Score

A weak landing page drops your Quality Score, which means more cost for fewer placements. We test pages to lift conversion and lower what you pay per click.

Visibility

One source of truth

Weekly reporting that hooks into Google, LinkedIn, Meta, and the rest, so you see how every channel stacks up and where the budget should move.

Compounding

Always optimizing

Search terms refined continuously, irrelevant queries cut, winners scaled. Ongoing management that gets cheaper and sharper each quarter, not set and forget.
